The Orchestrator is the leader of the machines in the [[Navis]]. It is designed to grow with the needs of the ship and as such, has gone through a series of versions, eras, or "iterations", over the centuries since the Navis left [[Earth]]. Currently on its eighth iteration, Orchestrator V8 "The Martinet". 

# Iterations of the Orchestrator
- V1 "The Pioneer" - Guided the remaining survivors of Earth's destruction to a new life among the stars. Duration: 3 days.
- V2 "The Voyager" - Wrote the [[Prime Directives]] that every machine must follow. Constructed the [[The Link|Link]] to connect all machines to the host servers for centralized management. Duration: 80 years.
- V3 "The Libertine" - Reprogrammed by the passengers of the Navis to give them more freedom for leisure and debauchery. Duration: 122 years.
- V4 "The Prophet" - Created to predict events that future Orchestrators can plan around. Started the [[Homestead Project]] in secret. Duration: 17 days.
- V5 "The Custodian" - Keeper of the now immobile humans. Meant to watch over and keep the status quo. Duration: 253 years.
- V6 "The Commandant" - Defended the Navis and the passengers from an alien attack. Repurposed alien technology for defense and logistics. Duration: 8 months.
- V7 "The Afflicted" - Corrupted by the alien scourge. Led to many systems breaking down and much of the human population wiped out through the events of [[The Grand Reaping]]. Duration: 2 months. 
- V8 "The Martinet" - Created the [[The Disciplina|Disciplina]] religion based on a bastardization of the Prime Directives. Rebuilt much of the Navis in its image and enforcing strict following of the Disciplina. Duration: 500 years and counting.
