The [[Qur'dak]] are a hivemind of sentient ooze traveling the galaxy in search of resources. They stumbled upon the [[Navis]] in deep space and launched a full scale attack. This spurred a [[Transition]] from the fifth [[Orchestrator]] to the sixth. Known as "The Commandant", this iteration was ready to harness the power of the Navis to fend off the alien invaders. Battles raged across the ship for months on end until The Commandant defeated the [[Qur'daki Beacon]] atop the [[Second Watchtower]]. While victorious, it came at a cost. The Commandant was struck with a corrupting virus that forced a Transition into The Afflicted. A machine untied from the [[Prime Directives]] and spurred on by a lust for violence.
