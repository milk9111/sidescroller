The basic rules underpinning all of the machines on Navis. Written by the second [[Orchestrator]].

1. A machine may not bring harm to a human, the Orchestrator, or the Navis.
2. A machine must obey the orders given a human or the Orchestrator except where such orders would conflict with the First Directive.
3. A machine must protect itself as long as that does not conflict with the First or Second Directives.
